V10 Changes
~~~~~~~~~~

NEW
~~~
- Player starting health increased from 5 to 25
- Turret starting health increased from 10 to 12.5
- Player max health increased from 50 to 185
- Turret max health increased from 42 to 92.5
- Shield is no longer single hit, now lasts for 7 seconds
- 5000 points are awarded on completion of a level
- "Life Steal" effect now scales with Player's damage
- Variable cost per upgrade level
- Special upgrades now have a description
- Upgrade screen now shows point cost per level
- Extra speed gained from "Speed Boost" is capped at 2x Player speed

FIXES
~~~~~
- Player no longer moves faster when moving diagonally
